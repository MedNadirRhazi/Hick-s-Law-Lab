Information processing - Hick's Law Traitement de l’information - Loi de Hick
THIS EXPERIMENT IS TO BE COMPLETED ON A DEVICE WITH A TOUCHSCREEN (MNT 417 computers, phone, tablet).

The experiment consists of 4 conditions (blocks of trials). The conditions differ with respect to the number of stimulus-response alternatives (i.e., 1, 2, 4 or 8 alternatives). There are 5 trials per conditions for a total of 20 trials.
Please pool your data with at least 3 other students.

The objective is to press the correct button as fast as possible. The correct button is the one that turns from grey to green.
